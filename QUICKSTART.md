## Troubleshooting

**No sound?**
- Check microphone permissions (should ask on first use)
- Increase volume slider
- Try a different microphone from the dropdown

**High latency?**
- Enable Ultra-Low Latency Mode
- Use wired headphones (not Bluetooth)
- Close other audio apps
- Try Chrome/Edge browser

**Feedback/echo?**
- ALWAYS use headphones, never speakers!
- Lower volume and boost

**Full troubleshooting guide:** See [README.md](./README.md#troubleshooting)
